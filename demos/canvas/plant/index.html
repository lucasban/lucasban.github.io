Care Instructions

  • Watering: Water your plant when the health drops. It gets thirsty over time (even when you're gone).
  • Growth: The plant grows slowly. Come back tomorrow to see new branches.
  • Wisdom: Click your plant's face daily to collect thoughts in your Leaf Journal.
  • Affection: Click the trunk to pet your plant! It loves the attention.
  • Visitors: Watch for ladybugs, butterflies, and bees. Tap them for bonuses!
  • Badges: Earn achievements by caring for your plant over time.
