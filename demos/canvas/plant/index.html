Digital Plant

A little persistent friend. It grows in real-time.

Age 0 Days
Health 100%
Plant a new seed (Reset)

Care Instructions

  • Watering: Water your plant when the health drops. It gets thirsty over time (even when you're gone).
  • Growth: The plant grows slowly. Come back tomorrow to see new branches.
  • Neglect: If health reaches 0%, the plant will wither. You'll need to plant a new seed.
