Flow Field

Particles flowing through Perlin noise fields

Click on the canvas to add attractors. Right-click to clear them.

About

This demo uses Perlin noise to create a flow field that guides particles across the canvas. Each particle follows the direction indicated by the noise value at its position, creating organic, flowing patterns.

Click anywhere on the canvas to add an attractor point that pulls particles toward it.
