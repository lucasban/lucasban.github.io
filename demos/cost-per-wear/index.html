About

Cost per wear helps you evaluate purchases by dividing the price by how many times you wear something. A $200 jacket worn 200 times costs $1 per wear - better value than a $20 shirt worn twice ($10/wear).

Use the Estimate Wears tab to figure out realistic wear counts based on how often you'd wear something and for how many seasons.
